Refund & Exchange Policy

At Rida Al Nissaj, we strive for perfection in every stitch. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Custom Made Garments

Due to the bespoke nature of our Thawps and Jubbas, items that have been tailored to your specific measurements are generally non-refundable unless there is a clear manufacturing defect or fitting error on our part.

Fabric Purchases

Uncut fabrics can be returned within 14 days of purchase, provided they are in their original condition and packaging.

Processing Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. Refunds will be processed to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.
